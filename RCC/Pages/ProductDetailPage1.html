About This Product

Jetboil's iconic low weight, low fuss, high-performance stove gets an upgrade!


Jetboil have updated their iconic Flash stove for 2018, remodelling the combustion and updating its materials to optimise efficiency, speed and precision. It's now their fastest-ever stove, with a 100 second boil time for a litre of water.

  • 1 litre FluxRing cooking cup with insulating cosy
  • Improved easy on-and-off vessel connection
  • Improved lid for better fit and function
  • New ergonomic design for easy hold and start
  • Thermochromatic, colour-change heat indicator
  • Convenient & reliable push-button igniter
  • Bottom cover doubles as a measuring cup and bowl
