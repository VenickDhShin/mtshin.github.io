About This Product

This Product is Perfect for touring, or weekend campers, the Rosedale 5pa sleeps up to five people in a tunnel design tent.


Part of the Prime Air (PA for short) collection from Outwell, the Rosedale 5PA offers a quality, lighter weight family structure, which can sleep up to five people. One of the many benefits of an Outwell tent, you get a mesh panel on each entry point, which is a massive bonus in hot or wet weather conditions. Furthermore, the Quick and Quiet technology on the doors, allows entry into the sleeping compartments without the use of loud zips. The Rosedale is ideal for touring, due to the inflatable nature, so it is quick and easy to set up and down, whilst the lightweight 70 denier fabric makes its highly portable.

  • Outtex 4000mm Flysheet, 70-Denier
  • Large windows with toggled-up curtains
  • 2 Premier Bedroom with generous space for up to 5 people
  • Sewn-in groundsheet for maximum comfort.
  • Two-zone design with shelter at the side.
  • Durable storm webbing straps
